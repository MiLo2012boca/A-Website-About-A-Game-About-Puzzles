A Website About A Game About Puzzles

A Game About Puzzles is a game where you solve puzzles, made by MiLo2012boca, an argentinian indie game developer. It has over 25 main levels and over 20 tutorial levels. In the game, you can push boxes, and also push TNTs, and you have to get to the door to beat the level.
